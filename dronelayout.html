DRONE TECHNOLOGY

Workshop Overview

This workshop introduces participants to the fundamental concepts, capabilities, and applications of drone technology. It aims to enhance engagement, capture unique footage, ensure safety, and create memorable experiences for attendees and viewers.

OBJECTIVE:

The objective of this workshop is to provide participants with hands-on experience in drone technology. Attendees will gain insights into drone operation, aerial photography, safety measures, and real-world applications.
